# Inspector Alerts for Slack

_How to start receiving Slack alerts for your Inspector issues_

Connect Inspector to your Slack to receive alerts when new issues appear in your production environments.

To connect find the "Set up alerts" in the Inspector Issues or Events view – or in your workspace settings.

![Connect to Slack in the Issues view](/images/workspace/nav-inspector-slack-highlighted.png)

_Connect to Slack in the Workspace Settings_

Press the `Connect to Slack` button and follow the instructions.

Avo Inspector will post new issues it finds in production hourly to the specified Slack channel.

The Inspector integration for Slack currently alerts on the following issues:

- Property is sometimes not provided
- Property is sent with inconsistent types
- New event seen that is not in the Tracking Plan
- New property seen that is not in the Tracking Plan
- Property type does not match the Tracking Plan
